Facebook Gaming Partner Program

The Facebook Gaming Partner Program is designed to help streamers take the next step in their streaming careers by offering exclusive benefits such as monetization options, early access features, and personalized support.

By applying, you can unlock new ways to grow your community and engage with your audience in exciting ways.

Key Benefits of Joining the Facebook Gaming Partner Program:
  • Monetization Options: Earn money through ads, fan subscriptions, and Stars from your followers.
  • Exclusive Support: Get access to a dedicated Partner Support team to help you with technical issues and strategy guidance.
  • Early Access to New Features: Be among the first to try out new features and tools designed to improve the streaming experience.
  • Content Promotion: Facebook Gaming may feature your streams to a broader audience through special promotions.
  • Custom Branding: Utilize custom badges, emotes, and other branding options to personalize your stream and build stronger fan connections.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with other top streamers, creators, and influencers to grow your presence in the gaming community.
